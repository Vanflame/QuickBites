Quickbites - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and password during registration. When placing an order, we collect your delivery address. We may also gather anonymized usage data to improve site performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to manage your account, fulfill orders, personalize your experience, and communicate important updates or promotions. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to store user preferences, authenticate sessions, and analyze traffic. You can modify your browser settings to manage cookie behavior.

4. Data Retention

Your information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services or meet legal requirements. You may request deletion of your account data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase by Google to manage authentication, data storage, and app functionality. Firebase may collect limited device and usage data (such as IP address or app events) as required for its services. All data shared with Firebase is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

We may also integrate analytics or payment providers who collect data under their own terms and privacy practices.

6. Data Security

We apply appropriate security measures including encrypted data transmission and password hashing. However, no platform can guarantee absolute security, so we encourage users to use strong, unique passwords.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. For requests or privacy concerns, contact us at support@quickbites.ph.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology, services, or regulations. Updates will be posted with a new effective date at the top of this page.
